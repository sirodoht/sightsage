Why you should get Sight Sage

Using GPS, Sight Sage pinpoints your location

With your permission and using your phone's or tablet's GPS Sight Sage pinpoint your location. After that Sight Sage shows you where you are on the map.

Places near you

After having your location Sight Sage can show you what places you can visit at the place you are. Museums, coffee houses, monuments, archeological sites that are near you! You can also get information about major sights from wikipedia almost instantly!

Where to go first?

You can pick what places you want to visit and then using shortest path algorithms a guided route is proposed!

We value your time

Since your time at a place can be limited, using our categories feature you can choose what kind of places you want to visit. Furthermore, you can add places to your favorites and track your history.

Bird's eye

Οur app uses the Bing Maps API, so you can view the city you are at from a bird's point of view!

Travel Bloggers

Using Sight Sage you can automatically get search results and learn what the travel bloggers have said about where you are.
